Electric Sit-Down Forklift

EE-rated forklift for barrel rack and IBC tote handling in classified areas.

Purpose: Moves barrel racks, IBC totes, and palletized loads throughout the facility. The EE electrical rating is required for operation in Class I, Division I classified areas where flammable vapors may be present.

Operator Certification Requirements

OSHA Requirement
All operators must be certified per OR-OSHA Division 2, Subdivision N (29 CFR 1910.178) before operating this equipment. Certification must be renewed every 3 years. Refresher training required following any accident or observed unsafe behavior.

Pre-Operation Inspection Checklist

  • Tires: no visible damage or excessive wear
  • Forks: not bent, cracked, or twisted
  • Mast: operates smoothly through full range
  • Horn: functional
  • Seatbelt: present and latches correctly
  • Battery level: sufficient for planned workload
  • Hydraulic lines: no visible leaks
  • Brakes: tested and functional before leaving charging area
