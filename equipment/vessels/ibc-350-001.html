Safety Notes

  • Grounding: Bond the tote cage to a designated facility ground before opening the top manway when handling flammable spirits.
  • PRV / Vent: Replace the pressure relief valve with an open candy-cane vent before any hot-fill CIP operations to prevent implosion.
  • Stability: Confirm tote is on a level surface and cage feet are seated before filling above 50% capacity.
