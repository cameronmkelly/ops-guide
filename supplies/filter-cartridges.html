Filter Cartridges

Depth pre-filter and membrane cartridges for spirit polishing and pre-bottling filtration.

Filter cartridges are single-use consumables for the cartridge filter housing. Monitor differential pressure during use — a significant pressure spike indicates a cartridge is blinding and must be replaced. Never reuse cartridges across different spirit batches.
