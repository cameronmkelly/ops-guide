Chemical Index

Safety information, dosing benchmarks, storage requirements, and compatibility for all facility chemicals.

Each chemical note summarizes the key information from the full Safety Data Sheet: hazards, first aid, storage requirements, dosing benchmarks, and material compatibility. Always consult the full SDS before using an unfamiliar chemical or in an emergency.

Chemical Handling Rule
Always add chemicals to water — never water to chemicals. Wear required PPE (gloves, goggles, apron) when handling any chemical in this guide.

Cleaning Chemicals

Alkaline Cleaner
Alkaline Detergent
Oxidizing alkaline cleaner for Tier 2 CIP. Removes organic soil, deodorizes, and sanitizes stainless steel equipment.
Acid Rinse
Citric Acid, Anhydrous
Food-grade acid for Tier 3 CIP and stainless steel passivation. Neutralizes alkaline residues and removes mineral scale.
Sanitizer
No-Rinse Sanitizer
Low-concentration sanitizer for food contact surfaces. Applied after CIP rinsing — no water rinse required.
