Calibration & Verification

Compliance Requirement
Alcohol meter calibration must be documented for TTB compliance purposes. Maintain a calibration log and perform verification checks with certified reference standards at the frequency specified by the manufacturer.
  • Calibrate at the start of each production day using a certified reference standard.
  • Clean the sample cell with RO water between each measurement to prevent carryover.
  • Log all calibration results and any out-of-tolerance findings.
