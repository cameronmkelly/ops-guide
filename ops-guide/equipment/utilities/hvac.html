HVAC & Exhaust System

Climate control, ventilation, and vapor exhaust for flammable liquid processing areas.

Purpose: Maintains safe temperature and humidity conditions in the facility while continuously removing flammable vapor from all processing areas. Proper function of the exhaust system is a life-safety requirement in any area where alcohol is handled.

Life Safety System
The exhaust system must be verified as operational before beginning any production activity. If the exhaust system is down, halt all operations involving open containers of alcohol until it is restored.

Maintenance Schedule

  • Monthly: Inspect and replace air filters. Check LEL sensor function.
  • Quarterly: Professional inspection of exhaust fan belts, bearings, and damper operation.
  • Annually: Full HVAC service including coil cleaning and refrigerant check.
