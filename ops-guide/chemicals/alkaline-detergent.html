Alkaline Detergent

Oxidizing alkaline cleaner for Tier 2 CIP of stainless steel equipment.

Purpose: Used for Phase 2 (Tier 2) CIP cleaning of stainless steel processing equipment. Provides powerful alkaline oxidizing action to remove organic soil, kill odor-causing bacteria, and deodorize surfaces. Environmentally benign — decomposes into oxygen, water, and soda ash.

Storage & Handling

  • Store between −25°C and +40°C in a strictly segregated area, away from strong acids and reducing agents.
  • Do not store on wooden pallets. Do not stack bags more than 8 high.
  • Store upright in the original container. Never return unused material to the original container.
  • FIFO rotation — test for active oxygen content after 1 year of storage.

Material Compatibility

Compatible: PVC, polyethylene, stainless steel (304L, 316, 316L), PTFE, glass, porcelain.

Incompatible — never mix with: Concentrated acids or alkalis, reducing agents, heavy metal salts, iron, copper, nickel. Natural and synthetic rubbers are also incompatible.

Safety & First Aid

Hazards
Strong oxidant. Corrosive to eyes, mucous membranes, and skin. Contact with combustible materials may cause fires.
  • Eye contact: Flush immediately with water for at least 15 minutes. Treat eyes first if both face and eyes are exposed.
  • Skin contact: Flush immediately with soap and water; remove contaminated clothing.
  • Ingestion: Do NOT induce vomiting. Rinse mouth and give large quantities of water.
  • Fire: Water is the preferred extinguishing agent.
